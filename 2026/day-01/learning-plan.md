## About DevOps Engineer

1. A DevOps Engineer is responsible for infrastructure as well as troubleshooting.
2. They collaborate with developers on day-to-day activities.
3. They are responsible for innovation and continuous improvement.
4. They handle deployments on a regular basis.
5. A DevOps Engineer should have strong technical understanding, troubleshooting skills, and focus on automation to reduce redundant work.  
   They also work with monitoring tools and cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and GCP.

   ---

   ## Understanding about Cloud Engineer

   1. A Cloud Engineer is responsible for designing, deploying, and managing applications and infrastructure on cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and GCP.
   2. They ensure systems are scalable, secure, cost-effective, and highly available.
   3. They closely work with cloud best practices, automation, monitoring, and troubleshooting when needed.
